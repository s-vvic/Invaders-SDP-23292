Welcome to the game! Here's how to play:

Controls

  • Player 1:
    • Move Left: A
    • Move Right: D
    • Move Up: W
    • Move Down: S
    • Shoot: Spacebar
  • Player 2 (if enabled):
    • Move Left: Left Arrow
    • Move Right: Right Arrow
    • Move Up: Up Arrow
    • Move Down: Down Arrow
    • Shoot: Enter

Scoring

Defeat enemies to earn points. Higher difficulty enemies yield more points.

  • Normal Enemy: 10 points
  • Special Enemy: 50 points
  • Boss: 1000 points

Enemies

Beware of various enemy types, each with unique attack patterns and health.

  • Basic Grunt: Standard enemy, easy to defeat.
  • Fast Attacker: Quick and agile, harder to hit.
  • Heavy Defender: Slow but heavily armored.
  • Bosses: Powerful enemies at the end of each level.

Items

Collect power-ups dropped by defeated enemies to gain an advantage.

  • Heal: Restores one life.
  • Shield: Grants temporary invincibility.
  • Stop: Freezes all enemies for a short duration.
  • Push: Pushes all enemies back.
  • Explode: Destroys all on-screen enemies.
  • Slow: Slows down enemy movement.
